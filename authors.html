Call for Papers

ICACCT-2022 is soliciting original, previously unpublished and high quality research papers addressing research challenges and advances in the tracks mentioned below. The maximum allowable length of the paper is 6 pages (including figures and references) for regular research articles strictly in IEEE DOUBLE COLUMN FORMAT. Articles will be Double-Blind Peer-Reviewed by three experts to decide its suitability for publication in the conference. Acceptance of papers will be communicated to authors by email.

Prospective authors are invited to register and submit the manuscripts of their original research contributions and review articles in the following areas:
